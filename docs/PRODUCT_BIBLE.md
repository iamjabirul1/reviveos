# SECTION 1 — Executive Summary

## One-Sentence Description

ReviveOS is an AI-powered dormant revenue recovery platform that scores stale leads, writes hyper-personalized win-back messages, and books meetings — with full human approval on every message sent.

## Elevator Pitches

### Investor Version
"68% of B2B leads go cold before closing. That's $1.3 million in dormant pipeline sitting in the average CRM. ReviveOS is the first AI platform purpose-built for pipeline recovery — it scores dead leads, writes hyper-personalized win-back messages using per-workspace AI that learns what converts, and books meetings with 100% human-in-the-loop approval. Our moat is a self-improving AI learning loop: the more messages a customer sends, the smarter their AI gets, making ReviveOS impossible to switch away from. We're targeting the $4.2B sales engagement market with a blue ocean approach — we don't compete with Outreach or Salesloft on new pipeline. We exclusively own the dead pipeline category."

### Customer Version
"You know those thousands of leads in your CRM that went cold? The no-shows, the 'not right now' objections, the proposals that went silent? ReviveOS uses AI to find the ones worth saving, writes personalized messages that feel hand-crafted, and puts meetings on your calendar — while you approve every single message. Most teams recover 15-30% of their dead pipeline within the first 30 days."

### Simplified Version (5 Seconds)
"ReviveOS turns your dead leads into booked meetings using AI — with human approval on every message."

## Product Vision

To become the world's default system for recovering dormant revenue, making it impossible for any business to leave money on the table in their CRM.

## Mission

Empower every sales team to recover the revenue they've already earned by re-engaging the leads that said "not now" — not "never."

## Problem Statement

B2B companies spend enormous amounts acquiring leads, but 68% go cold before converting. Most teams simply move on, treating dead pipeline as sunk cost. There is no purpose-built system for recovering this revenue. Generic outreach tools aren't designed for win-back scenarios, and manual follow-up doesn't scale. As a result, the average company has $1.3 million in dormant pipeline — money they've already paid to acquire that's being left on the table.

## Why This Product Matters Now

1. **AI maturity**: Large language models are finally good enough to write personalized, contextual sales copy that doesn't feel robotic.
2. **CRM data abundance**: Every company has years of lead data sitting in their CRM, creating a rich dataset for AI-powered recovery.
3. **Economic pressure**: In a tighter economy, companies need to extract more value from existing assets rather than spending on new lead acquisition.
4. **Speed-to-lead research**: Studies from MIT, Harvard Business Review, and Salesforce confirm that 78% of customers buy from the first company that responds, and response speed is the #1 conversion predictor.
5. **Trust gap with AI**: Buyers distrust fully automated AI outreach. ReviveOS's human-in-the-loop model is perfectly timed — it delivers AI scale with human quality control.

## Market Timing

The sales engagement market ($4.2B) is saturated with tools fighting for *new* pipeline. Nobody owns the *dead* pipeline recovery category. ReviveOS is the first mover in a category we're creating. The convergence of AI capability, economic pressure, and CRM data abundance makes this the ideal moment to launch.

# SECTION 2 — Problem Landscape

## The Core Problem: Dead Pipeline Is a Trillion-Dollar Waste

Every B2B company has the same dirty secret: most of their leads die before converting, and nobody does anything about it. The reasons are structural:

1. **SDRs chase new leads** because quota incentivizes fresh pipeline, not revival.
2. **CRMs archive dead leads** into a graveyard nobody revisits.
3. **Manual follow-up doesn't scale** — writing personalized win-back emails for 5,000 stale leads would take weeks.
4. **Generic sequences don't work** — "Just checking in" emails get ignored because they show zero understanding of why the lead went cold.
5. **There's no system** specifically designed for pipeline recovery.

## Real-World Scenarios of Customer Frustration

### Scenario 1: The Agency Owner with 3,000 Dead Leads
*Rachel runs a marketing agency. Over 2 years she's accumulated 3,000 leads in her CRM from webinars, referrals, and ad campaigns. About 60% are untouched or went cold after initial outreach. She knows there's gold in there but her 3-person team is focused on active deals. She'd need to hire a full-time SDR just to work through old leads — and even then, they'd send generic "just checking in" emails that get 2% reply rates.*

**Pain**: She's paying for lead acquisition but only converting 30% of what she pays for. The rest decays.

**What she needs**: A system that can scan those 3,000 leads, identify which ones are worth re-engaging, write personalized messages that reference their specific situation, and let her approve before sending.

### Scenario 2: The SaaS VP of Sales with 800 Closed-Lost Deals
*James manages a 12-person sales team at a mid-market SaaS company. They have 800 closed-lost opportunities from the past 18 months. The reasons range from "timing," "budget," and "went with competitor" to "ghosted." His reps are on quota for new business — they don't get paid for reopening old deals. But James knows that at least 20% of those "timing" objections are now past their original blocker.*

**Pain**: 800 leads that cost $50-200 each to acquire are just sitting there. At a $15K average deal size, even a 5% win-back rate = $600K in recovered revenue.

**What he needs**: AI that understands why each deal was lost, writes messages that acknowledge what's changed since then, and generates different messaging for "timing" vs "budget" vs "went with competitor" objections.

### Scenario 3: The Coach Who Had 50 No-Shows Last Quarter
*Maria is a business coach who books discovery calls through her website. Last quarter she had 50 no-shows. She knows from experience that no-shows often still have intent — they just got busy or nervous. But writing individual follow-up emails that don't sound passive-aggressive is time-consuming, and she doesn't want to seem desperate.*

**Pain**: Each discovery call is worth $2,000 in potential coaching revenue. 50 no-shows = $100K in lost potential. But the emotional labor of following up gracefully is exhausting.

**What she needs**: AI that writes no-show follow-ups with the right tone — acknowledging the gap without guilt-tripping, offering a low-friction way to reschedule.

## Economic Impact

For a typical B2B company:
- **Cost per lead acquired**: $30-200 (depending on channel)
- **Leads that go cold annually**: 500-5,000
- **Wasted acquisition spend**: $15K-$1M/year
- **Recovery potential at 15% win-back**: $50K-$500K in recovered revenue
- **ROI of ReviveOS**: 10-50× the subscription cost

## Emotional Pain Points

Beyond the economics, there are deep emotional pain points:

- **Guilt**: Sales leaders know they should be working old leads but don't have the bandwidth.
- **Frustration**: Writing personalized follow-ups at scale feels impossible.
- **Fear of looking bad**: Nobody wants to send "just checking in" emails that make their brand look desperate.
- **Analysis paralysis**: With thousands of old leads, where do you even start?
- **Trust issues with AI**: People want AI help but don't trust it to represent their brand without oversight.

## Operational Pain Points

- **CRM clutter**: Dead leads clog pipelines and make reporting inaccurate.
- **No prioritization**: All dead leads look the same — there's no way to know which ones are actually worth re-engaging.
- **Compliance risk**: Following up without proper consent tracking or suppression lists creates legal exposure (GDPR, CAN-SPAM).
- **No attribution**: Even when old leads convert, teams can't trace it back to the recovery effort.

Score and prioritize thousands of stale leads so I know where to focus my limited time and resources.

Give me confidence that I'm not wasting time on leads that will never convert, and reassure me that I'm not missing hidden opportunities.

Let me show my team/board that I have a data-driven approach to pipeline management, not just gut feelings.

# SECTION 7 — Blue Ocean Strategy

## The Blue Ocean: Dead Pipeline Recovery

The entire sales engagement industry fights over the same red ocean: **new business outreach**. Outreach, Salesloft, Apollo, and dozens of others compete on features, integrations, and price for the same workflow: find new leads → sequence them → close.

ReviveOS creates new market space by exclusively targeting **dead pipeline** — leads that already exist in CRMs but are being ignored. This isn't a feature within an existing category; it's an entirely new workflow.

## Eliminate / Reduce / Raise / Create Framework

### ELIMINATE (factors the industry competes on that we don't need)
- ❌ Lead database / prospecting tools (we use leads you already own)
- ❌ Multi-user seat licensing (our per-workspace model is simpler)
- ❌ Complex CRM sync requirements for basic functionality (CSV upload works)
- ❌ Autodial / phone features (not needed for re-engagement)
- ❌ LinkedIn automation (compliance risk, not our focus)

### REDUCE (factors we offer but below industry standard)
- ↓ Sequencing complexity (our playbooks are simpler and purpose-built)
- ↓ Integration ecosystem (we focus on the most common CRMs, not all of them)
- ↓ Reporting complexity (focused on recovery metrics, not general sales analytics)

### RAISE (factors we offer well above industry standard)
- ↑ AI personalization depth (company research, enrichment data, brand context, learning loop)
- ↑ Human approval controls (100% approval rate, edit capability, suppression)
- ↑ Revenue attribution (lead → campaign → playbook → revenue tracking)
- ↑ Compliance controls (suppression lists, consent tracking, jurisdiction awareness)
- ↑ Time to value (15 minutes to first campaign, not weeks of setup)

### CREATE (factors the industry never offered)
- ⭐ **Revival Scoring Engine** — Purpose-built lead scoring for dead pipeline recovery (0-100)
- ⭐ **Per-Workspace AI Learning Loop** — AI that gets smarter with every message outcome
- ⭐ **AI Confidence Scores** — Transparency into how confident the AI is for each message
- ⭐ **A/B Testing with AI** — Automatic variant generation for every message
- ⭐ **Business Context Onboarding** — AI learns your brand voice, differentiators, and audience before writing
- ⭐ **Playbook-based Recovery** — Pre-built workflows for specific recovery scenarios (no-show, closed-lost, stale, proposal, dormant customer)

## Value Innovation

Traditional sales engagement platforms optimize for **volume** (send more emails to more people). ReviveOS optimizes for **relevance** (send the right message to the right lead at the right time with the right tone).

This is value innovation because we simultaneously:
1. **Lower cost** — Cheaper than hiring SDRs or buying Outreach licenses
2. **Increase differentiation** — No competitor offers AI learning, revival scoring, or human-in-the-loop approval

## Feature 2: Revival Scoring Engine

### Problem It Solves
When you have 5,000 stale leads, you don't know where to start. Which leads are actually worth re-engaging? Which ones should be left alone?

### How It Works
The scoring algorithm (defined in `src/lib/scoring.ts`) uses a deterministic, rule-based approach:

**Base score**: 50 points (neutral starting position)

**Score modifiers**:
| Signal | Points | Logic |
|--------|--------|-------|
| No-show in last 30 days | +25 | Recent no-show = high intent |
| Timing-based closed-lost reason | +20 | "Not now" / "timing" / "budget" = revisitable |
| Last contact 14-120 days ago | +15 | Sweet spot window for re-engagement |
| Lead value > $1,000 | +15 | High-value opportunity |
| Prior engagement notes available | +10 | More context for personalization |
| Last contact > 120 days ago | -5 | Getting stale |
| Old lead, never contacted | -15 | Low probability of recovery |
| Do-not-contact flag | → 0 (Suppress) | Compliance |
| No email or phone | → 0 (Suppress) | Can't reach them |

**Bucket assignment**:
| Score Range | Bucket | Action |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| 75-100 | Revive Now | Immediate outreach recommended |
| 50-74 | Review First | Manual review before outreach |
| 25-49 | Nurture Later | Long-term drip, not immediate |
| 0-24 | Suppress | Do not contact |

**Additional outputs**:
- **Best angle**: The primary reason this lead is revivable (e.g., "Recent no-show — high intent signal")
- **Best channel**: Email or SMS based on available contact info
- **Risk flag**: Warnings like "Previously opted out" or "Low revival probability"
- **Suggested CTA**: book_call, reply, claim_offer, or answer_question

### Example Use Case
Lead: Sarah Martinez, VP Marketing at TechCorp
- No-show 15 days ago (+25)
- Lead value: $5,000 (+15)
- Last contacted 20 days ago (+15)
- Has detailed notes (+10)
- **Score: 50 + 25 + 15 + 15 + 10 = 115 → clamped to 100**
- **Bucket: Revive Now**
- **Best angle**: "Recent no-show — high intent signal"
- **Suggested CTA**: reply (because no-show)

"Within seconds of import, every lead gets a revival score from 0-100. You'll know exactly which leads are worth your time and which ones to skip."

# SECTION 18 — Frequently Asked Questions

## Customer FAQs

**Q: Does ReviveOS send messages automatically?**
A: No. Every message requires your approval before sending. You can review, edit, or reject any message in the approval queue. AI generates the messages; you maintain full control.

**Q: What if I don't have a CRM?**
A: No problem. You can export leads from any system (spreadsheets, email lists, webinar signups) as a CSV file and import them directly. No CRM integration required.

**Q: How does the AI know about my business?**
A: During onboarding, you provide your company details, target audience, brand voice, key differentiators, and topics to avoid. This context is used in every AI-generated message to ensure it sounds like YOU, not a robot.

**Q: Will leads know I'm using AI?**
A: Messages are sent from your email address and sound like they were hand-written. The AI uses your brand voice and references specific details about each lead's company. Recipients have no way to tell it's AI-generated.

**Q: What happens if a lead asks to be removed?**
A: Use the "Suppress Lead" button in the approval queue to immediately add them to your suppression list. They'll be marked as do-not-contact and excluded from all future campaigns.

**Q: How is ReviveOS different from Outreach or Salesloft?**
A: Outreach and Salesloft are designed for new business sequences. ReviveOS is purpose-built for recovering dead pipeline. We have revival-specific scoring, AI that learns from your outcomes, human approval on every message, and revenue attribution. Different tools for different use cases.

**Q: Can I use ReviveOS alongside my existing sales tools?**
A: Absolutely. ReviveOS complements tools like Outreach, Salesloft, and HubSpot. Your team uses existing tools for new business; ReviveOS handles the warm re-engagement of old leads.

**Q: How quickly will I see results?**
A: Most users send their first campaign within 15 minutes of signing up. Replies typically start within 24-48 hours. Most teams recover their first deal within 14 days.

## Sales FAQs

**Q: What's the typical sales cycle?**
A: Self-serve signups convert same-day. Demo-to-close is typically 1-2 weeks for SMBs, 4-6 weeks for mid-market.

**Q: What's the average contract value?**
A: Self-serve averages $79/month (Growth plan). Enterprise/agency deals can be $99-199/month.

**Q: Who is NOT a good fit?**
A: Companies with fewer than 100 leads in their CRM, companies that don't do outbound at all, and companies in industries where cold outreach is prohibited (e.g., certain regulated industries).

**Q: What's the competitive win rate?**
A: Against "do nothing" (our main competitor), win rate is high when we can demonstrate ROI math. Against Outreach/Salesloft, we position as complementary rather than competitive.

## Technical FAQs

**Q: What AI model does ReviveOS use?**
A: Google Gemini 3 Flash Preview via a secure AI gateway. The model is selected for its speed, quality, and cost-effectiveness for sales copy generation.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All data is stored in a SOC2-compliant cloud database with row-level security. Each workspace's data is completely isolated. We never share data between workspaces or use your data to train models for other customers.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes. Leads, messages, and campaign data can be exported via CSV at any time. Revenue attribution data and AI insights are available through the dashboard.

**Q: What email sending service do you use?**
A: Resend for email delivery. This provides high deliverability rates, open/click tracking, and bounce handling.

**Q: How does the AI Learning Loop work technically?**
A: When you record an outcome (reply, booking, deal won), we extract the message's metadata (tone, CTA, angle, subject pattern, variant) and update aggregated win rates in your workspace's AI insights. These insights are then injected into future AI prompts as "proven patterns" with specific win rates, instructing the AI to lean toward approaches that have worked for your business.

A: Yes. The free plan includes 500 leads, 1 campaign, 3 playbooks, and 10 AI calls per day. It's limited but fully functional for trying the product.

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**

**Q: Is there a money-back guarantee?**
A: While we don't offer a formal money-back guarantee, you can cancel at any time with no penalty. The free plan lets you validate the product before upgrading.

**Q: What happens when I hit my lead limit?**
A: You'll see a notification bar showing your current usage. You won't be able to import more leads until you upgrade your plan or delete existing leads.

# Appendix A — Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Revival Score** | A 0-100 score indicating how likely a lead is to re-engage |
| **Revival Bucket** | One of four categories: Revive Now, Review First, Nurture Later, Suppress |
| **Playbook** | A reusable messaging strategy template with tone, CTA, channel, and sequence |
| **Approval Queue** | The interface where users review and approve/edit/reject AI-generated messages |
| **AI Learning Loop** | The system that feeds message outcomes back to improve future AI generation |
| **AI Confidence Score** | A 0-100 score indicating how much historical data supports the AI's approach |
| **Business Context** | Company details, brand voice, and audience information used to personalize AI output |
| **Revenue Attribution** | Tracking recovered revenue back to specific campaigns, playbooks, and messages |
| **Suppression List** | A list of leads marked as do-not-contact |
| **Enrichment** | AI-powered research that adds company and industry data to lead records |
| **Variant** | One of two A/B test versions of a message (A = standard, B = experimental) |
